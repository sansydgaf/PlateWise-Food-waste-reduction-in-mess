# PlateWise

AI-powered smart mess sustainability platform reducing hostel food waste through food preference profiling, attendance prediction, demand forecasting, waste analytics, and gamified carbon footprint rewards.

## Problem

Hostel messes experience food wastage because of skipped meals, menu dissatisfaction, repetitive food patterns, and weak demand prediction.

## Features

- Food preference profiling
- Menu voting
- Attendance prediction
- AI demand forecasting
- Plate waste tracking
- Carbon footprint analytics
- Sustainability leaderboard
- Rewards ecosystem
